On boarding introduction

You are now on the homepage of Corendon Travel Buddy.

Travel specifications

Here you can set your travel specifications and see all the people you've matched with.

You can set your travel specification by filling out your start and end date and the city you're travelling to. The system will then look for other users with that same travel specification.

Matching page

Underneath the travel specifications you'll find all your current matches.

If you have no current matches you'll see this on your screen otherwise you'll find a list of all the people who you've been matched with.

The navigation bar.

Here you can quickly swap between the different tabs.

In the all results tab you can view all of your current matches.

In the recently viewed tab you'll see the profile you've most recently visited.

In the matched tab you can find the people you're currently matched with.

You can find the people you've favorited in the favorites tab.

Profile cards

When you click on of these matches you'll find some extra information about that user.

You can click on the profile button to view even more information and then accept their invitation or block them.
