Homepage

This is the homepage of Corendon Travel Buddy.

Travel specifications

Here you can set your travel specifications and see all the people you've matched with.

When you set your travel specifications the system will look for other people with the same destination and similar interests.

Matching

Underneath the travel specifications you'll find all your current matches.

The navigation bar.

Here you can quickly swap between the different tabs.

In the all results tab you can view all of your current matches.

In the recently viewed tab you'll see the profile you've most recently visited.

In the matched tab you can find the people you're currently matched with.

You can find the people you've favored in the favorites tab.

Profile cards

On the profile cards you have 2 different options. You can select match or profile

The match will send a notification to the other person notifying them you are now matched with each other.

The profile button will send you to the profile of the person you selected. Here you can view in detail who the person is.
