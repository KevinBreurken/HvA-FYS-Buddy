What is Fasten Your Seatbelts?

Fasten Your Seatbelts (or also known as FYS for short), is the name of this project and is carried out on behalf of Corendon, a Dutch holding specialized in flight holidays.

What is the main goal of the Fasten Your Seatbelts project?

The purpose of the development of the Fasten Your Seatbelts project is to bring single people together allowing for traveling in groups rather than alone.

How can I reach out to Corendon?

Corendon has its own website containing all of the information related to contact. Please refer to their main website at: https://www.corendon.nl/contact.

Application guide

In this section will be explained how to navigate through the application as well as its available features.

Navigation

Navigation is possible through taking a look at the available navigation buttons located at the top side of the screen (next to the Corendon banner).

What is the matching system?

The application allows for search of other travelers who might have similar interests and might potentially be a match.


The search can be specified by applying different kinds of filters which can be displayed by pressing on the filters button located at the top right.


A traveler who's profile matches, might be selected down below.


Each traveler has his or her own description in relation to their location, available dates, activities and other useful data. This information can be found within each of the cards down below.
